Understanding Our Support Types

Our support types are designed to match the level of academic monitoring and intervention required by each student. The number of assessments, feedback frequency, and level of tutor involvement increase with higher support levels.

More frequent assessments allow tutors to evaluate understanding after teaching specific concepts, adjust teaching methods where necessary, and provide detailed feedback. This process requires additional tutor time outside of lesson delivery.

Support types are not forced. However, they are a strong recommendation based on the student’s current performance and academic needs. In some cases, students may be struggling and require closer monitoring to ensure meaningful progress.

Integrity & Payment Approach

We encourage new clients to begin with a pay-per-lesson approach to build integrity and mutual trust. With time and consistency, clients may transition to monthly arrangements, governed by a contract to protect both parties.
